I joined the RTB (Real-Time Bidding) team, where I enjoyed working side by side with engineers and data scientists.

Creatives are one of the key parts of RTB as it's the only UI with the user. What’s in front of a very complex system to show the best creatives customised to each user.

When I joined the team, creatives where barely functional and not optimised.

I started testing several options to find what fitted better for each case.

What started trying to combine good-looking creatives with data-driven decisions, became many different options.

Since the project started, we created over 800 creatives for 14 different sizes.
