Oxfam Trailwalker

100km, 4 people, 1 cause

Trailwalker is a team challenge to fight against poverty. It consists of four member teams walking together 100km in less than 32 hours. Some former colleagues at Trovit participated in the event. I proposed to design something special for the occasion. I made my part creating the logo and other elements for the team, which where used in all the technical equipment.
