Oxfam Trailwalker

100km, 4 people, 1 cause

Trailwalker is a team challenge to fight against poverty, where four member teams walk together 100km. Some former colleagues at Trovit participated. I proposed to design something special for the occasion, creating the logo and other elements, which where used in all the technical equipment.

Christmas Party 2015

Every Christmas is time to celebrate. In 2015, we threw a big party at Hard Rock Cafe Barcelona, with customized swag and some little surprises everyone enjoyed.

Hackathon 2015

In Trovit's 2nd Hackathon, I joined a team where we created the Trovit Quiz. It was a game about Trovit employees, to be shown in the video wall and multiple monitors we had spread across the office. The idea was that anyone could play just watching the screens. I focused in visual design and game mechanics. We finished the game in time and it has been working happily since then.
