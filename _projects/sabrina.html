Research

We wanted to collect qualitative and quantitative data, so we used several ways of research. Surveys, interviews, user profile, study competitors, track user behaviour, among others.

Analysis

We collected a lot of data, and I was thrilled to study trends and behaviour patterns. Synthesise all the data drove me to identify user needs, pain points and challenges, to validate assumptions and discover new ways. We also did a prioritisation of issues, based on impact, criticality and most frequent actions.

Design System

To offer an excellent and consistent experience, we built a Design System that helped us streamline all scenarios.
