--- layout: project name: app title: Delivery App for drivers subtitle: MOBILE APP | STORY TELLING date: 2017-03-02 description: The last-mile delivery app used by drivers. The intention was to create an app easy to use and avoid repetition. So, we did several interviews and spent some days as drivers to better understand the challenge. The app was a never-ending project as we improve it and add new functionalities while the needs were growing. The information showed and reported in the app was synchronized with the software used by managers that control all deliveries. full_width: true ---

Interviews and Surveys

I started with some research. I stepped back and gathered insights and different kind of information listening to the current users (drivers), collecting the max num of responses and real answers.

User Persona

To generating empathy and understanding our users, we create a user persona. We make informed decisions capturing relevant information while discovering insights in a simple and visual map.

Analysis

To develop a better solution, we create an affinity map to visualize our users. This way, we define core problems and identify opportunities. We discover the main pain points, needs, most valuable things for the user, their wishes, among others.

Ideate and validate

With the knowledge from the analysis and conversations with the team, I sketched low-fidelity wireframes for the app. After a couple of paper iterations, I had the general idea ready.

Final Design

We simplify flows when building the new app to help drivers go ahead as quickly as possible with no distractions.
