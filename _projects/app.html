The intention was to create an app easy to use and avoid repetition. So, we did several interviews and spent some days as drivers to better understand the challenge. The app was a never-ending project as we improve it and add new functionalities while the needs were growing. The information showed and reported in the app was synchronized with the software used by managers that control all deliveries. full_width: true ---
